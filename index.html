Discover Warsaw, a city of contrasts.

Top three activities to do at Warsaw

Visit the Lazienki Museum in Warsaw

Visit the Lazienki Museum in Warsaw and be transported to a world of royal splendor! Discover masterpieces of art, elegant interiors and picturesque gardens from the 18th and 19th centuries. This is a unique journey through Polish history, showcasing cultural heritage in a unique way.

Discover Warsaw's magical Old Town!

Discover Warsaw's magical Old Town! Stroll through charming cobblestone streets, surrounded by historic townhouses and picturesque squares. Immerse yourself in history by exploring the reconstructed buildings after war damage. It's a place full of atmosphere, where every stone tells a unique story of the capital. Bring the past to life and feel the magic of the Old Town!

Visit Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw

Enter your senses into the world of the impressive Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw! Discover the architectural masterpiece that dominates the city skyline. The interiors hide not only concert halls, but also fascinating museums and exhibitions. This is the symbolic heart of Warsaw, filled with history and culture. Enter this monumental building and experience a unique artistic and scientific heritage!
